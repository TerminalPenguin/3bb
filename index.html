The Hunger Games

        
The Hunger Games is a dystopian novel by Suzanne Collins, where the oppressive Capitol forces twelve districts to send two tributes to fight in a brutal, televised battle for survival. The story follows Katniss Everdeen, who volunteers in place of her sister and must navigate the deadly arena. The first edition was published in 2008 and became a bestseller, launching a trilogy that inspired blockbuster films
